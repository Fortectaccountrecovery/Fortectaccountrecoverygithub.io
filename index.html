Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover my Fortect account?

Use the password recovery option on the Fortect login page, verify your registered email address, and follow the password reset instructions to regain access.

Why can't I log in to my Fortect account?

Login issues may be caused by incorrect credentials, forgotten passwords, browser cache problems, or temporary account verification issues. Verify your login information before attempting to sign in again.

Can I change my account email address?

Yes. After signing in to your account, you can update your account information if the option is available or contact support for additional assistance.

How do I manage my Fortect subscription?

After logging in, you can review your subscription, update payment information, renew your plan, manage automatic renewal, and access billing history.
